CLIMATE
The climate is the same all year round.
Salar de Uyuni has a cold desert climate.
GEOLOGY
Salar de Uyuni is the world's largest salt flat, or playa, at over 10,000 square kilometers in area.
FORMATION
It was formed when Lago Minchin-a prehistoric lake one covering the majority of southwest Bolivia-dried up.
